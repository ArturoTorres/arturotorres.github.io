Project Portfolio

CHAPEL

High-resolution climate change projections for Luxembourg. Project funded by the Government of the Grand-Duchy of Luxembourg.

Temporal Uncertainty Propagation Analysis

This research constitutes a contribution to facilitate dimensioning and design of the required infrastructure for decision-making in sustainable urban water management as a “Blue-Green-Grey” vision for the cities of the future, by paying specific attention to model input uncertainties and their propagation to model outputs as used by decision makers.

Clim4Vitis

Climate change impact mitigation for European viticulture Clim4Vitis is a three-year Horizon 2020 project funded by the European Union Research and Innovation programme.

EOTSA

The Earth Observation Time Series Analysis Toolbox for Proba-V data, was a project funded by the European Space Agency and the Luxembourg Institute of Science and Technology. Co-development by 52° North (Germany).

QUICS

Quantifying Uncertainty in Integrated Catchment Studies (QUICS) was an EU funded FP7 Marie Curie Initial Training Network (ITN) which ran for 4 years, starting in 2014.
