QGIS, Quantum GIS

QGIS, or Quantum GIS, is an open-source Geographic Information System software that allows users to create, edit, visualize, and analyze geospatial data. It offers a wide range of tools for mapping, spatial analysis, and data management, making it a powerful and accessible solution for GIS tasks. With its user-friendly interface and extensive plugin support, QGIS provides a versatile platform for individuals and organizations to work with geographic information and gain valuable insights for various applications.
